Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 19, 2026

Overview

Haggly ("we," "our," or "us") operates the website haggly.io. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our service. We are committed to keeping your experience private and transparent.

Information We Collect

We do not require user accounts. You can use Haggly without signing up, logging in, or providing any personal information. We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personally identifiable information through the core phrase tool.

Preset phrase translations are processed in your browser from static translation data. If you use custom translation or generated negotiation translation features, the text you enter may be sent directly from your browser to third-party translation services to return translated output.

Automatically Collected Information

Like most websites, our hosting provider (GitHub Pages) may automatically collect certain technical information when you visit haggly.io, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Referring URL
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Device type (mobile, desktop, tablet)

This information is used for analytics and to ensure the website functions properly. It is not used to identify individual users.

Cookies and Local Storage

Haggly may store limited preference data in your browser (for example, language and consent preferences) using local storage. Our advertising partner (Google AdSense) and analytics providers may also set cookies or similar identifiers on your device. These technologies are used to:

  • Serve relevant advertisements
  • Measure advertising performance
  • Prevent the same ads from being shown repeatedly
  • Understand how users interact with ad content

You can manage cookie preferences through browser settings and the consent controls on our site. Blocking cookies or storage may affect some features.

Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on haggly.io. Google AdSense uses cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and other websites on the internet. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to haggly.io and/or other sites on the internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings. Alternatively, you can opt out of third-party vendor cookies for personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info/choices.

Service Workers and Offline Data

Haggly is a Progressive Web App (PWA) that uses service workers to enable offline functionality. When you visit haggly.io, your browser may cache website files locally on your device so the app works without an internet connection. This cached data stays on your device and is not transmitted to us. You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to other websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of other sites. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any linked websites you visit.

Children's Privacy

Haggly is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information associated with our website. Our site is served over HTTPS, and we do not store personal user data on our servers. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at our contact page.
